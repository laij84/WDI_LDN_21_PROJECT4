ProDiary is a daily task-management tool to help you level-up your productivity, using competition and gamification. ProDiary will quantify your productivity in "financial" terms, helping you re-evaluate how your time is spent, so you can plan your activities more wisely.
Categorize your tasks by productivity level. Get "paid" for completing productive tasks, and "spend" your earnings to be unproductive. Compete with other users to reach the top 10 on the leaderboard.
